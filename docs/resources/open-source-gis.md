# Open Source GIS Resources

> Curated collection of open-access tools and datasets for participatory GIS (PPGIS) research. Focus areas: urban environments, mobility, and ecosystem services.

**Source:** [Participatory Mapping Institute](https://participatorymapping.org/resources/open-source-gis/)

---

## PPGIS Datasets

### GREENTRAVEL PPGIS Survey
Point data on pleasant and unpleasant places as well as greenery preferences along everyday active mobility routes. Collected from five European cities: Helsinki, London, Copenhagen, Munich, Las Palmas.

- **Link:** [Zenodo](https://zenodo.org/records/17866393)
- **Related:** [[mobility-research]], [[european-cities]]

### Urban Biodiversity Parks Citizen Survey
Dataset from Turku, Finland neighborhoods assessing nature-based solutions and participatory management activities.

- **Link:** [Zenodo DOI](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.18232358)
- **Related:** [[nature-based-solutions]], [[citizen-science]]

### PPGIS Survey: Me and Nature in My Everyday Life
Studied residents' nature experiences in Finland across Turku, Tampere, and Lappeenranta.

- **Link:** [Zenodo DOI](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.15744490)
- **Related:** [[nature-experiences]], [[finland-research]]

### Lifestyles and Preferences of Urban Residents' Everyday Mobility
Mobility habits and preferences dataset from Finnish cities (Espoo, Lahti, Oulu).

- **Link:** [Zenodo](https://zenodo.org/records/16760854)
- **Related:** [[mobility-research]], [[urban-planning]]

### PPGIS Survey: Ecosystem Services Across Europe
Data from 13 study sites in ten European countries collected in 2015-16.

- **URN:** `urn:nbn:fi:att:2fc777d5-e7f0-4efd-9560-5bfd292fed73`
- **Related:** [[ecosystem-services]], [[european-cities]]

## Tools & Software

### Hupmobile's Participatory Tools
Digital and traditional methods for understanding urban dweller perspectives on sustainable mobility.

- **Link:** [participatory.tools](https://participatory.tools/)
- **Type:** Web platform
- **Related:** [[participatory-tools]], [[sustainable-mobility]]

### Synthetic PPGIS Data Generation
Context-aware method that learns from previously collected PPGIS data and creates realistic datasets for methodological development.

- **Article:** [MethodsX (2022)](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.mex.2022.101871)
- **Type:** Methodology / Data generation
- **Related:** [[synthetic-data]], [[research-methods]]

### IASM: Individualized Activity Space Modeler
GIS toolbox in Python for analyzing and describing human mobility patterns and modeling activity spaces.

- **GitHub:** [SOFTX_2018_35](https://github.com/ElsevierSoftwareX/SOFTX_2018_35)
- **Article:** [SoftwareX (2018)](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.softx.2018.04.005)
- **Language:** Python
- **Related:** [[python-gis]], [[mobility-analysis]]
